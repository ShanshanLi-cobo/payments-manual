The Cobo Payments AI Assistant is an intelligent chatbot embedded in Cobo Portal. It helps you complete initial setup, explore core payment features, and answer product questions.

## Where to Find It

The Cobo Payments AI Assistant is located in the bottom-left corner of Cobo Portal. You can click it at any time to open or collapse the panel.

## Onboarding Guide (Starter Test Plan)

If you are using the Starter Test plan for the first time, the Cobo Payments AI Assistant will automatically walk you through a complete pay-in and payout experience to help you get familiar with the core payment flow:

1. **Create an order**: The AI Assistant guides you through creating a test payment order.
2. **Complete the payment**: You can transfer funds yourself, or have the AI Assistant claim test tokens on your behalf to complete the payment.
3. **View the balance**: Once the order is settled, the AI Assistant displays your account balance.
4. **Initiate a payout**: The AI Assistant guides you through transferring funds to a specified address.

<Note>
The onboarding guide uses testnet and USDT test tokens. No real funds are involved at any point.
</Note>

## Everyday Use

After completing the onboarding guide (or for Starter Live and Enterprise plan users), the Cobo Payments AI Assistant serves as a day-to-day support entry point. It can help you:

- Learn about the features and usage of Cobo Payments
- Get answers to API integration and Webhook questions
- Receive personalized integration recommendations based on your business scenario

## Data Usage and Security

The Cobo Payments AI Assistant is part of Cobo AI. For details on its design principles, data usage and security measures, and capability boundaries, see [Cobo AI Introduction](https://manuals.cobo.com/en/ai/introduction).
